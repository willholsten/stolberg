Eat & Drink

The Stolberg takes pride in our diverse food and beverage offering to cater to all walks of life. We boast a vegan and vegetarian friendly menu with plenty of gluten free options. An extensive wine list along with craft beer and cocktails we have just what your pallet desires whether it be to go with a steak or snack with some friends.

  • Eat

Our menu offers gluten free, dairy free and vegan options.
